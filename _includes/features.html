Community Driven

CAPSLOCK was created for students by students, to make sure it continues we encourage all members to be part of the community organization, lectures or even just being active on the community chat group.

Plug and Play

Joining the our developer community is 100% free, no fees or waiting lists, just come eat pizza and code away. We are also very aware of the students academic schedule and plan our event and projects accordingly.

Collaboration at Core

We believe that teamwork is a key factor for success, it enables the students to overcome challenges that they may not have been able to individually without the help of others. We work hard to embrace teamwork and collaboration in our events and projects.
